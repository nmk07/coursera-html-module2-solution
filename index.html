Importance of Tricolor of Indian National Flag

Saffron

The Saffron colour at the top of the flag stands for courage and selflessness. It stands for renunciation and absolution of the self or 'Ego'. The color represents the courage of those soldiers who have dedicated their lives to protecting their motherland.

White

The white color in the middle of the Indian National flag stands for peace, honesty, and purity. The colour stands for knowledge and cleanliness as well. Its political significance is the constant reminder that the leaders need to maintain peace and harmony throughout.

Green

The green colour at the bottom of the flag represents fertility, faith, and prosperity. It is a colour that stands for life, success, and happiness. Politically, it is again a reminder to the leaders to protect their motherland from foreign invasion and attacks.
